Since the board now spans five platforms, here's the shorthand:

- **AfterQuery** — heavy on frontier "ML Expert" research roles and deep accounting sub-specialties. Lots of $150-200/hr.

- **Turing** — engineering and AI-training roles; pay is often listed as "varies/TBD" and set per project.
- **Invisible** — high-volume language alignment work across a huge range of world languages; the widest pay range, entry-level friendly at the low end.

## How to Apply

The same fundamentals keep working:

1. **Match your real experience to the role.** They verify credentials. Don't apply for MD or bar-admitted roles without the qualifications.
2. **Finish the whole application.** The AI interview is part of it — most people who start don't finish, and finishing alone puts you ahead of the pack.
3. **Be specific.** "Eight years in M&A at a bulge-bracket bank" beats "experienced in finance."
4. **Set up payment properly** (Stripe Connect for most platforms) so you're not chasing your first payout.
5. **Be patient with matching.** Getting accepted isn't the same as immediate work — projects arrive in waves.
